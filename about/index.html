I am a Ph.D. candidate of Geoscience at the University of South Florida, working in the Geodesy Laboratory. I hold a B.E. and M.E. in Geodesy and Geomatics from Wuhan University (China).

My research interests include Geodesy and Geophysics, particularly applied to study of marine-terminating glacier calving and crustal deformation with geodetic data.
