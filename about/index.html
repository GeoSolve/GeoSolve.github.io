About

I am a graduate student of geoscience at the University of South Florida, working in the Geodesy Laboratory. I hold a B.E and M.E. in Geodesy and Geomatics from Wuhan University (China).

My research interests include Geodesy and Geophysics, particularly applied to study of marine-terminating glacier calving and shallow water crustal motion with geodetic data.
