  • July 2019: New paper (Xie et al., 2019) published online. We developed new methods to reduce errors in topography mapping with terrestrial radar interferometry. Now we are able to map elevation changes with decimeter precision over a wide area. Applying the new methods to data obtained in 2016, we found that icebergs are inhibited from moving into the fjord of Jakobshavn Isbræ during an unusually long period, due to buttressing force by a tightly packed pro-glacial mélange wedge.
